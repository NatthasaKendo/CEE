Welcome to Laugh Against Chaosity !

Laugh Against Chaosity is a web-based online card game inspired by Cards Against Humanity. A game where players fill in the blank for the funniest answer. Though the game is a little bit offensive, where's the fun without some jokes? I hope you enjoy it!

Rules & How to play

Every round, one player will be the judge.
Other players will try to come up with the funniest joke to win a score from the judge.


1) The Judge chooses Black Card to the question of the round. (or create his own question)


2) Other players choose White Cards that they think it’s the funniest in the Black Card that the judge chose in one minute.


3) After the timer runs out or the host presses “Skip”, every players' answers will be shown to everyone but no one will know who chose each answer. The judge will have to choose his favorite answer. The player who chose that answer will score one point.

And the next round begins when another player will become the judge and repeat the game step until every player has become the judge for a set number of rounds.


At the end of the game, there will be a leaderboard that shows every player’s score and the player who scored the most point wins!

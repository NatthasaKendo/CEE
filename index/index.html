Welcome to Laugh Against Chaosity !

Laugh Against Chaosity is a web-based online card game inspired by Cards Against Humanity. A game where players fill in the blank for the funniest answer. Though the game is a little bit offensive, where's the fun without some jokes? I hope you enjoy it!

Rules & How to play

Every round, one player will be the judge. Other players will try to come up with the funniest joke to win a score from the judge.

At the start of the round the judge will have to choose one of three Black Cards (Randomed by the game) to the question of the round. The judge can also type in his own prompt to be the Black Card and specify the number of white cards that needed to be picked by selecting the number of a blank.

After that, other players will have to choose one of three White Cards (Randomed by the game) that they think it’s the funniest in the Black Card that the judge chose. Players can also type in their own answer or upload a picture to be an answer if they want to.

After the timer runs out or the host presses “Skip”, every players' answers will be shown to everyone but no one will know who chose each answer. The judge will have to choose his favorite answer. The player who chose that answer will score one point.

And the next round begins when another player will become the judge and repeat the game step.

At the end of the game, there will be a leaderboard that shows every player’s score and the player who scored the most point wins!
